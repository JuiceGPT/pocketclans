Welcome to the Elemental Realm

The Shinkai: A World in Harmony

From the dawn of time, the Shinkai—a hidden world parallel to our own—was a place of perfect harmony. Seven elemental forces - Rock, Water, Air, Fire, Lightning, Plant, and Ice - lived in balance, maintaining peace across both realms. But chaos struck, scattering the elemental creatures into our world and plunging both into disarray.

Your Mission: Restore Balance

Your mission, brave adventurer, is to restore balance by recruiting elemental beings. With the roles of BOSS, TANK, WITCHER, SCOUT, DOC, and NOOB, each clan member plays a unique part in the battle to reunite their kin and save both worlds. Face them in ritual duels, prove your worth, and become the legendary Yōkai-Sama—the master of all clans.

Join the Journey

Every victory brings us closer to harmony and a brighter future. Join us on this epic journey and bring the elemental clans back to the Shinkai!
